About

Nikolai Roldugin is a Russian amateur photographer based in Toronto, who is frequently traveling around the world. In his jorneys he captures architectural and historical monuments, nature and people in general who catch his eye. The drive behind his works is his constant passion for new and deep immersion in the world around. This web-site is a result of Nikolai's interest in design and three months of HTML and CSS learning. It was made as a personal photo blog using the most basic web design technologies.
